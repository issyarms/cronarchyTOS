Workflow and Process

  • ETA of products
    • Depending on the complexity of the commisioned item and my general motivation, the time until completion may greatly vary. If there are any significant delays, I will notify the customer.
    • All commision types will include a sketch approval stage (and any other consequent stages depending on what was commisioned), at which time I will send any wips to the customer via ko-fi. Until it has been approved, progress will be paused.
    • Upon completion, I will send the customer a full resolution PNG of the commisioned item and the .clip file via Ko-Fi (I will also send via email or Discord upon request)
  • Revisions
    • At the sketch approval stage, any major changes or revisions must be made at this point; this includes any significant changes to a character's pose or body shape, or redrawing parts of the image.
    • If I make any errors in the character design, accessories, or any other visual elements that specified in the provided visual references, I will make these edits at no additional cost.
